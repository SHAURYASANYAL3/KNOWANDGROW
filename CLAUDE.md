# Content & Communication Rules

Always use:

* trauma-informed communication
* non-stigmatizing language
* compassionate tone
* evidence-based claims

Avoid labels such as:

* "criminal child"
* "drug addict"
* "dangerous youth"

Preferred alternatives:

* "at-risk youth"
* "youth experiencing substance abuse"
* "vulnerable adolescents"

* prioritize government/university/WHO/UNICEF/NIMHANS data

* Government reports

* NIMHANS
