# Requirements Document

## 1. Project Vision
Create a youth-safe, trauma-informed digital platform for awareness and education on:
- Mental health
- Digital safety
- Drug prevention
- Sex education
- Juvenile vulnerability

## 2. Core Objectives
1. **Prevention-focused education** - Provide evidence-based information to reduce harm
2. **Youth-centric design** - Calm, trustworthy, accessible interface
3. **Ethical communication** - Non-stigmatizing language, trauma-informed approach
4. **Legal compliance** - Adhere to Indian laws (POCSO, Juvenile Justice, DPDPA)
5. **Data privacy** - Minimal data collection, child privacy protection

## 3. Target Audience
- Ages 13-25
- Students, young professionals, vulnerable youth
- Parents, educators, caregivers
- Partners: schools, mental health professionals, law enforcement (for reporting)

## 4. Functional Requirements
1. **Core Sections**:
   - Home
   - About
   - Focus Areas (Mental Health, Digital Safety, Drug Awareness, Sex Education, Juvenile Prevention)
   - Resources
   - Blog
   - Volunteer
   - Contact
   - Emergency Help
   - FAQ
   - Privacy & Safety

2. **User Flow**:
   - Clear navigation between sections
   - Consistent CTA for help/resources
   - Emergency contact accessibility

3. **Content Requirements**:
   - Age-appropriate, culturally sensitive content
   - Evidence-based information with proper citations
   - Non-stigmatizing language
   - Trauma-informed communication

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
1. **Performance**: Page load < 2 seconds
2. **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
3. **Security**: HTTPS, secure forms, anti-spam, minimal data collection
4. **Mobile Responsiveness**: Fully responsive design
5. **Internationalization**: Support for multiple languages

## 6. Technical Stack
- Frontend: Next.js + TailwindCSS
- Hosting: Vercel
- Forms: Formspree
- Database: Supabase/PostgreSQL
- Authentication: Optional, minimal

## 7. Content Sources
- Government reports (NCRB, NIMHANS)
- WHO/UNICEF publications
- Peer-reviewed research
- Trusted NGOs

## 8. Compliance Requirements
- POCSO Act adherence
- DPDPA 2023 compliance
- Child data protection
- No legal advice provision
- Regular legal review

## 9. Tone & Style Guide
- Calm, trustworthy, non-judgmental
- Compassionate and educational
- Avoid fearmongering or sensationalism
- Use "at-risk youth" instead of stigmatizing terms

## 10. Future Expansion
- School workshops
- Digital safety programs
- Volunteer management system
- Multilingual support
