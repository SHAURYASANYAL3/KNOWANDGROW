# Screen Plans Document

## Home Screen
**Purpose:** Primary entry point for users
**Key Elements:**
- Hero banner with mission statement
- Navigation menu (linked to all main sections)
- Quick links (Emergency Help, Contact)
- Featured content carousel (recent resources/stories)
**User Flow:**
1. Enter URL or app
2. Explore core sections
3. Access emergency help if needed

## Focus Areas Screen (Directory)
**Purpose:** Central hub for topic-specific content
**Key Elements:**
- Filterable list of focus areas (Mental Health, Digital Safety, etc.)
- Badges for trending topics
- Search bar
- "Learn More" buttons for each area
**User Flow:**
1. Choose topic from dropdown
2. View subtopics/resources
3. Navigate to specific resources

## Resource Center Screen
**Purpose:** Collection of educational materials
**Key Elements:**
- Filter by type (articles, videos, guides)
- Searchable database
- Download buttons
- Categories matching focus areas
**User Flow:**
1. Search or browse resources
2. Filter by relevance
3. Download or save content

## Emergency Help Screen
**Purpose:** Crisis support hub
**Key Elements:**
- Clear CTA to contact helpline
- Location-based resource mapping
- "Tell Someone" button
- Anonymous reporting option
**User Flow:**
1. Recognize crisis
2. Initiate help request
3. Connect with support

## Volunteer Portal Screen
**Purpose:** Engagement for supporters
**Key Elements:**
- Registration form
- Skill-matching dropdown
- Shift scheduling calendar
- Impact tracking dashboard
**User Flow:**
1. Sign up as volunteer
2. Match with cause
3. Schedule participation

## Blog/Resource Library
**Purpose:** Educational content
**Key Elements:
- Date-sorted articles
- Topic tags
- Comment section
- Email newsletter signup
**User Flow:
1. Browse latest posts
2. Explore by category
3. Subscribe for updates

## About Section
**Purpose:** Build trust
**Key Elements:
- Mission statement
- Team photos
- Partner logos
- Legal compliance badges
**User Flow:
1. Read about us
2. Verify credentials
3. Contact team
