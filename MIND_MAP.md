## Supporting Elements
- Communities: Online Forums, Peer Support Groups
- Education: Awareness Campaigns, School Workshops
- Technology: Secure Forms, Antispam Protection, Child Data Protection
- Legal: Compliance with POCSO, Juvenile Justice Act, DPDPA 2023
- Ethics: Non-stigmatizing Language, Trauma-Informed Communication

## Resource Flow
Research → Content Creation → Awareness Campaigns → Intervention → Support → Monitoring

## Ethical Guardrails
- Prevention-focused messaging
- Rehabilitation orientation
- Zero tolerance for sensationalism
- Strict data privacy protocols
