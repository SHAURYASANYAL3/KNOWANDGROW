Emergency help

Immediate guidance for unsafe or urgent situations

If you are in danger, contact local emergency services first. Use the guidance below to find safe support if you feel threatened, overwhelmed, or unsafe.

Step 1: Stay safe

Find a secure place, reach out to someone you trust, and avoid leaving yourself alone if you are in danger.

Step 2: Reach out

Use available helplines, school counselors, family members, or community support groups to connect with help.

Step 3: Seek trusted support

Do not share personal details on unsafe platforms. Prefer verified sources and local professionals whenever possible.
