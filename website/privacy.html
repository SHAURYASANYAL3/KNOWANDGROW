Privacy

Data protection and safe engagement

We respect privacy and collect only minimal data needed to support inquiries and volunteer interest. This information is used solely for project communication and community support.

What we collect

  • Contact details shared through our forms.
  • Volunteer interest information.
  • Anonymous usage information for website improvement.

How we use data

We use contact details only to respond to messages and to invite volunteers to engage safely. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

Safety commitment

We avoid collecting sensitive information and provide content without requiring login. For any request to delete information, contact us via email.
